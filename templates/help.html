How do I start a quiz?

From the home screen, select a course and module, then choose "Start Quiz." Questions will appear one at a time.

Can I skip questions?

Yes, you can click "Skip Question" to move to the next one without answering.

How are scores calculated?

Scores are based on the number of correct answers out of the total questions attempted.

Where can I review missed questions?

After completing a quiz, click "Review Missed" from the results page or access them via the Scoreboard.

How do I change the course?

Click "Select Course" from the homepage or the module selection screen to switch between courses.
